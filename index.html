Reimagining the Future: A Product Manager’s Lens on AI Innovation

A Product Manager (PM) is responsible for guiding the success of a product and leading the cross-functional team responsible for improving it. They act as the bridge between business, technology, and user experience.

Key Responsibilities:
🔹 Define product vision and strategy based on market needs and company goals.
🔹Gather and prioritize requirements from customers, stakeholders, and competitive analysis.
🔹Collaborate with engineering, design, marketing, and sales to deliver product features.
🔹Own the product roadmap and lifecycle—from concept to launch and iteration.
🔹Measure success through KPIs like customer adoption, retention, and ROI.
